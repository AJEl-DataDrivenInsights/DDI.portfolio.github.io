Zillow ZHVI in Excel

This began with the original raw data that was taken from Kaggle. Gaps in the data led me to begin with values beginning in 2009 instead of 2000. I identified two categories which I called "Northeast" and "Mid-Atlantic". I wanted to identify locations in the US where I would consider moving, and identify how the ZHVI (Zillow Home Value Index) changed over time with respect to average home value. How was that impacted during the Recession around 2009 and 2010? How was that value impacted during the pandemic? Were there home markets that experienced significant growth or loss with regard to home values? Then, I was able to use that to compare to home markets where I already live in an attemtpt to explain what small or large growth in the home value index might mean to someone looking to buy or sell. I did not introduce average loan rates into the equation, but that would likely lend more insight to this exercise as a whole. I sought to answer some of these questions during this analysis.
