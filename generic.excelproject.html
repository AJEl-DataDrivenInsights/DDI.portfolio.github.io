This project began with the original raw data that was taken from Kaggle. Gaps in the data changed the start of our dataset from 2009 instead of 2000. I identified two categories which I called "Northeast" and "Mid-Atlantic". I wanted to identify locations I would consider moving, and identify how the ZHVI (Zillow Home Value Index) changed over time with respect to average home value. How was that impacted during the Recession around 2009 and 2010? How was that value impacted during the pandemic? We sought to answer some of those questions posed during this analysis.
